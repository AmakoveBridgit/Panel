Experience at Akirachix

My experience at Akirachix has been fascinating because i have learned
different diversities by having to interact with them.The hospitality offered
by the big sisters is welcoming,I have got to learn new things each day and thats
so interesting .I have also improved my confidence and am able to open up before anyone.

Teamwork

Teamwork is really encouraged at Akirachix.
Most of class work done especially the non technical
ones are mostly group work.Through this most of us get know
and understand each other.

Time management

Time management is a major key in life.In Akirachix we
are taught how to be time managers,we are encouraged to
finish our assigned duties on time and also wake up an hour
before class time to prepare ourselves well.

Prepared to be great people in future

In Akirachix,we are taught how communication skills ,we learn our values and perspectives. We are taught how to carry ourselves out and how to apply for interviews and jobs.
